What is Mead

Mead, or "honey wine," is a fermented beverage where the alcohol is derived primarily from honey. Beer gets its fermentable sugars from grain, wine from grapes, and mead from honey. Mead has been around for thousands of years and is arguably the world's oldest fermentable beverage. Just add water and the wild yeast present in the honey itself will begin spontaneously fermenting.

At Two Seasons Meadery we've combined modern brewing techniques with this ancient beverage.
