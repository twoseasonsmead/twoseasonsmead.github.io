A new meadery in Anchorage, Alaska

We are now officially open and serving mead four days a week at our Meadery in Anchorage, Alaska. We specialize in crafting unique, delicious meads that are drinkable for any occasion. We currently have six meads available for tastings or growler fills:

  • Sitka Delight: Dry Sitka Spruce Tip Metheglin
  • Tesserae: Dry Mosaic Dry-Hopped Metheglin
  • Cyser Soze: Off-Dry Cyser (Cider/Mead hybrid)
  • Meadarita: Semi-sweet Lime-Agave Melomel
  • Kazimiera: Sweet Lithuanian Style Metheglin
  • Rock the Razzbah: Semi-sweet Raspberry Melomel

At Two Seasons we want you to fall in love with mead the same way we have. The meadery is open:

  • Thursday: 4 - 8pm
  • Friday: 4 - 8pm
  • Saturday: noon - 8pm
  • Sunday: 2 - 6pm
